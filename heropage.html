Dear students/guardians

Congratulate all SEE graduates on your grand success in the SEE.

It is very immense to congratulate all SEE graduates on your grand success in the SEE, and I am equally delighted to welcome you all for your further academic journey at BFC. BFC has undergone its challenging phase quite successfully. At a time when unhealthy practices are trying to commercialize education, BFC has stood, within its short span of life, as a model pillar to prove that quality education can be inculcated in the students with moderate fees in a viable atmosphere.

We are committed to make BFC as the first choice of the selective candidates in the next few years. Leadership, Communication skill, and management abilities are the three trinity of the age. Collective effort of parents, students, faculty members, and the college management have equal share in the career building of any student. The role of all four is equal and profound.

Because of its commitment to impact both knowledge and skill among the students, BFC has set its foot on the summit of fame and popularity within a short span of time. With best wishes in the days to come, the scholars of BFC are waiting that they would be the real scholars in their respective fields.
